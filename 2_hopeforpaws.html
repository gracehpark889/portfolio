Hope for Paws

Hope for Paws is a non-profit organization that seeks to rescue and raise awareness for abandoned animals by partnering with local no-kill shelters and utilizing donations for the use of animals


My Role: User Experience & Interface Design, User Research
Tools Used: Adobe XD, Sketch, Invision, Photoshop
Timeframe: 2 weeks


The Problem

Individuals have limited resources and lack trust to donate to non-profits.


The Solution

Redesign website to be simplistic, transparent, and intuitive to prevent potential barriers that would hinder users from contributing.

Research

To understand the pain points that users experience when landing on a nonprofit website, a thorough interview plan was written out to gain insight on potential doners. We wanted to hear about interviewees':

      1. General Experience with non-profits
      2. Motivations to donate
      3. Barriers that would prevent individuals to contribute

Key Takeaways

Three main keypoints were discovered in the understanding of the motiviation behind why a use would, or would not, donate to a specific non-profit.
